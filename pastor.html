Palma L. Bennett

Palma L. Bennett served pastoral ministries in Florida, Texas, Tennessee and his
native state, North Carolina. He has preached revivals or leadership seminars in
South Carolina, Maryland, Massachusetts, Alabama, Florida and, in the
countries of Poland and Jamaica.

In addition to attending East Tennessee University, Lincoln Memorial
University, and the Graduate Seminary of Phillips University, he earned degrees
from Milligan University and Emmanuel Christian Seminary (formerly known
as Emmanuel School of Religion).

Palma and his wife Helen (Dink) live in Johnson City and have one son, Todd.
